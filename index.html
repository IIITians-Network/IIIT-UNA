    IIIT UNA


    FOUNDATION

    Indian Institute of Information Technology, UNA, Himachal Pradesh, popularly known as IIITU is a joint venture of the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, the Govt. of Himachal Pradesh, with Industries in Public-Private Partnership model, in 2014. Only two branches, i.e. CSE and ECE were introduced at that time. From 2017 IT branch was also introduced. Currently all the three branches are functioning properly.

    STUDENT LIFE

    The students have started various tech clubs as well as some event management and some cultural clubs to improve their skills. These clubs will also help in their personality developent as well. These clubs are solely run by the seniors under the mentorship of IIITU teachers.
